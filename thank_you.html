General

What is Yeva?
Yeva seeks to build relationships between newer caregivers and more experienced ones. By leveraging the knowledge and experience of someone who has been there before, we support and guide people along the journey.
Why are you building Yeva?
Yeva is being built by caregivers for caregivers. We want to support and guide caregivers along the journey. But more importantly, we want to make the caregiving journey as enjoyable and meaningful as possible.
Who is building Yeva?
The business team is Andrew Bright, who was a caregiver for his mother, and Dhruv Vasishtha, who is a caregiver for his father. In addition, we have a team of experienced caregivers and social workers composed of people who are currently walking the journey or who have in the past.
How can I help?
Yeva is being built by caregivers for caregivers. Tell us about your caregiving experience at caregivers@yeva.co and help us make Yeva the best resources for caregivers.
What disease areas are you focused on?
As we are building Yeva, we are currently focused on Alzheimer's and Dementia caregivers. As we grow, we want to support caregivers for any disease.
What does Yeva cost?
We are on a mission to make Yeva free for caregivers and paid for by health systems. At Yeva, we believe that caregivers should have access to the support and guidance they need without having to incur any more expense.
