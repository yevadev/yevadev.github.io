The Caregiver Crisis

People are growing older, retirement savings are shrinking, and the number of Alzheimer's and Dementia patients is expected to grow by 40% by 2025. The burden of care is increasingly falling on untrained family members who are busier and more overwhelmed than ever before.

We built Yeva for a Start


As caregivers, we know how overwhelming caring for a loved one can be. We, like most caregivers, had almost no experience, little free time, and few people for support. But, there are millions of caregivers who have been there before and are willing to help. We created Yeva to make the guidance of experienced caregivers accessible to everyone everywhere. Let's take this caregiver journey together.
