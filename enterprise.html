Hospice Benefits

Hospice organizations have reached out to Yeva as changes under MACRA and the ACA tie reimbursements to HCAHPS surveys, CMS Star ratings, and family caregiver experience. Hospice organizations can use Yeva to engage and support caregivers with personalized training and education. Hospices can also utilize Yeva to deliver necessary non-medical information and free up their nurses to focus on medical tasks, improving front line staff experience as well.

Home Health Benefits

Multiple studies from Johns Hopkins, NYU, and the University of Minnesota demonstrate that caregiver engagement enables patients to age at home for longer periods of times. Yeva enables best in class support for home health agencies who have a similar goal of enabling their customers to age at home with dignity and support for as long as possible.

Alzheimer's and Dementia Foundation Benefits

Yeva has presented its caregiver engagement research to the National Alzheimer's Association and other leading Alzheimer's & Dementia and family caregiver foundations. We provide our technology to foundations with mentor programs who want to ensure that their volunteers are able to answer all their constituents' questions with the best content, information, and resources. Yeva also provides data analytics services to foundations who are interested in better engaging their members.
